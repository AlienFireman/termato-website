Security & privacy

Privacy and security, at the core.

Privacy and security aren't a feature in Termato — they're the foundation. The architecture is built so your work never has to be trusted to anyone but you.

01 No middleman, no Termato server

Your code, your sessions, and your history never pass through us — there is nothing in the middle to intercept, log, or breach.

02 Your data never leaves your machine

Everything lives on hardware you control. We couldn't see your data if we wanted to, because it never reaches us in the first place.

03 Straight to the source

When you use AI, requests go directly to your provider under your own account — no extra hop, no broker, no copy kept anywhere.

04 Reachable from anywhere — but only by you

Open your workspace from any device through your own private endpoint, while your machine exposes no inbound ports for anyone to find or scan. The encrypted tunnel only ever reaches out.

05 A serious front door

Everything sits behind a hardened login wall with brute-force protection, cryptographically signed sessions, and optional IP allow-listing — for the app and your live previews alike.

06 Runs only on hardware you control

No cloud, no telemetry, no analytics. Put it on a server, a desktop or a laptop — Termato runs where you choose and answers to no one else.
